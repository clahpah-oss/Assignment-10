The Legend of Zelda

Breath of the Wild

A New Era of Open-World Adventure

Released in 2017 for the Nintendo Switch and Wii U, Breath of the Wild completely redefined the Zelda series and open-world gaming with its breathtaking freedom, physics-driven gameplay, and a massive, living version of Hyrule waiting to be explored.

Explore Freely

Climb towers and mountains, paraglide across valleys, solve over 120 shrines, and discover secrets in every corner of Hyrule.

Survive & Create

Hunt, cook, craft weapons and elixirs, and use the environment itself as a tool to defeat enemies in creative ways.
